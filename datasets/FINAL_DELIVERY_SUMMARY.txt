IMPORTANT NOTES

TRAINING DATA vs LOOKUP DATABASE
Training data (X_train.csv): Scaled numbers for AI training
Lookup database: Original values with food names for users
Do not mix them up

CLASS IMBALANCE
Food dataset has severe imbalance (Low_Risk only 0.8%)
Must use class_weight='balanced' in models
Use F1-Score, not accuracy
